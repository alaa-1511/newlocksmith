Digital & Electronic Door Locks.

What is the difference?

Fumbling for keys with groceries in your hand is a thing of the past. We specialize in the installation and programming of modern Digital Door Locks and Electronic Door Locks.

Digital Door Locks (Keypads): perfect for families with kids or rental properties. You can set a PIN code for entry, meaning no more lost keys and no need for spare keys under the doormat.

Electronic Smart Locks: These connect to your home’s Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. You can lock and unlock your door from your smartphone, receive alerts when someone enters, and even integrate them with your home security system.
